External contributions aren't welcome on the Lanius compiler (yet).

There are a couple reasons why this is true.

Firstly, I do not yet have a plan on dealing with a flood of AI contributions. While I trust myself to use AI in a way that accords with my values,
other well-intentioned developers may use AI in a way that I don't personally find acceptable.

Secondly, I do not think the project is in a mature enough state to accept the contributions of others, even if they are hand-crafted. I don't want someone to
contribute a ton of code only for me to change language features or design goals and for their contributions to become worthless.

So for these two reasons Lanius is closed to outside contributions, and will likely always be to some degree. If contributions ever open up it will likely
open up based on a system like https://github.com/mitchellh/vouch.
